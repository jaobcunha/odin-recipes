Lasagna

History

Here we will learn some about the famous recipe Lasagna.

Lasagna was invented circa 1208 by Josef Lasagna in the city of New Hamster - England. Josef discovered the dish while trying to effectively store his ingredients like sheets of paper.

Josef however didn't realize he wasn't storing his ingredients in the closet. It was the oven. About 2 hours later Josef found the dish again but this time, it was different. This time... It was lasagna.

Ingredients

  • One Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Oregano
  • One animal's meat

Steps

  1. Turn the oven on
  2. Stack ingredients on dish
  3. Put the dish in
  4. Wait like 2 seconds
  5. Done!
